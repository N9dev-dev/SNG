 Simple Name Gen 1.8 

Simple Name Generator is a utility tool used for re-naming, specialized in generating nicks for Simple Survival, as the Simple Survival server uses a /nick format that isn't supported by the original website BirdFlop.

Why Use SNG?

Here are some reasons why you should make SNG your main nick-naming tool.

EASY

Compared to other tools, SNG is a very beginner friendly tool. Everything is crystal clear.

QUICK

You can generate a /nick command in less than 30 seconds. So what are you waiting? give it a go!

COMPATIBLE

SNG is made to support a huge amount of formats, and if you find a format that isn't available, please contact me!
